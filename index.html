Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this AI Age Calculator?

Life stages are based on developmental psychology and biological milestones, helping you understand where you are in your overall journey.

Can I use this for my baby's age in months?

Yes! Our tool provides detailed stats including total months, weeks, and days, making it perfect for tracking growth milestones.
